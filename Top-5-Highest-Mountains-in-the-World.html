Yankari Game Reserve

  • DESCRIPTION:

    Yankari Game Reserve is a large wildlife park located in Bauchi State, in northeastern Nigeria. Its location in the heartland of the West African savanna makes it a unique way for tourists and holidaymakers to watch wildlife in its natural habitat. It is home to the remaining Elephants and Lions in Nigeria. It is under the management of Bauchi State Government.
